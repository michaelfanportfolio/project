Case Study

Campaign Page Optimization
(Citi, HSBC & Standard Chartered)

01 Project Overview

Project Type
Promotional Landing Page UX
Company
SingSaver (MoneyHero Group)
Timeline
May–June 2024
Team
Marketing, Compliance, UI Engineering

02 Problem

Business Goal

  • Maximize credit card applications during high-traffic, time-sensitive campaigns
  • Achieve quarterly acquisition targets

Key Challenges

  • Dense legal T&Cs placed directly on the page
  • Poor visibility of reward differences across campaigns
  • High cognitive load for users

User Impact

  • Difficulty comparing campaign offers
  • Decision paralysis during selection
  • Increased drop-off in application flow

Business Impact

  • Lower conversion rates despite high traffic
  • Inefficient use of paid ad spend

03 Research and Insights

Cognitive Overload

Users could not easily distinguish between the welcome gift, exclusive platform gifts, and the qualifying spending requirements.

Visual Clutter

Legal disclaimers were fighting for visual attention alongside the primary conversion buttons, creating a hierarchy nightmare.

User Expectation

Users expected clear reward values and simple, structured timelines to understand exactly how to claim their gifts without reading a legal manual.

04 Design Strategy

Progressive Disclosure

Decouple marketing copy from heavy compliance text to serve both business conversion goals and legal requirements simultaneously.

Visual Comparison

Translate text-heavy rewards into easily scannable, iconography-driven tiers to accelerate cognitive processing.

Component Modularity

Build a flexible template that can be rapidly deployed for different banking partners to scale campaign operations.

05 Solution

Scannable Reward Tiers

Designed a highly visual campaign template highlighting reward tiers (e.g., Tier 1 vs. Tier 2 spending requirements) using clear icons and distinct color grouping.

T&C Accordions

Placed heavy legal and compliance text inside expandable accordions at the bottom of the page, keeping the main interaction flow clean while fully satisfying the Compliance team.

Sticky Conversion Bar

Added a persistent "Apply Now" bar on mobile to capture intent at any scrolling depth, drastically reducing the interaction cost to convert.

06 Impact

Significantly increased the Campaign Click-Through Rate (CTR), maximizing the ROI on marketing ad spend.

Reduced user time-to-conversion by simplifying the visual decision-making process.

Sped up internal design deployment times by providing a modular, reusable campaign template, establishing a new operational standard for the marketing team.
