Case Study

COVID-19 Travel Insurance
Tactical Comparison Page

01 Project Overview

Project Type
Tactical Response & Comparison UX
Company
MoneyHero — Hong Kong
Timeline
2020
Team
Cross-functional teams: Product, Insurance, and Marketing

02 Problem

Post-pandemic travelers faced extreme anxiety regarding sudden flight cancellations and changing entry requirements for popular destinations like Japan and Thailand. The emotional stakes of travel were at an all-time high, yet the insurance shopping experience remained cold and transactional.

The business needed to rapidly deploy a high-converting tactical page to demystify complex COVID-19 insurance terms while effectively aggregating and comparing over 30 different market offerings in a highly volatile landscape. Speed to market was critical — every week of delay meant lost affiliate revenue and users defaulting to incumbent competitors.

03 Research and Insights

Entry Requirements
Confusion:

Users struggled to understand destination-specific entry mandates, such as minimum vaccine doses and PCR test validity, creating massive pre-purchase friction.

Financial Safety Priority:

Travelers heavily prioritized financial safety nets over standard coverage, specifically looking for mandatory quarantine cash allowances and medical expense limits.

Frictionless Policy
Comparison:

Customers demanded a frictionless comparison process to decide quickly between single-trip and annual insurance policies without reading fine print.

Competitive analysis of comparable markets (Singapore, Australia) revealed that the leading performers all shared one UX trait: they surfaced the most anxiety-relieving information first — quarantine coverage and medical limits — before presenting price. MoneyHero's legacy approach led with price, triggering price-sensitivity anxiety before establishing coverage trust.

04 Design Strategy

SEO & Acquisition Architecture

Structured the web architecture strictly around SEO standards — optimizing semantic tags and strategic keywords — and aligned the layout with social media campaign strategies to capture high-intent traffic and reduce the initial bounce rate. Every page section was mapped to a specific search intent query.

Accelerated Decision-Making

Enhanced the layout and UI elements to speed up page scannability. By surfacing key comparison data immediately, the design reduces the user's page view time and helps them make faster purchasing decisions — targeting a sub-5-minute purchase journey from landing to policy selection.

Drop-off Reduction via Product Listing

Redesigned the product listing experience to make plan comparison frictionless. This intuitive comparison model clearly highlights options to easily apply, directly targeting and reducing application drop-off rates. COVID-specific coverage attributes (quarantine cash, medical limits) are visually prioritised above standard metrics.

05 Solution

5-Minute Purchase Flow Optimization

Streamlined the user flow to ensure customers could browse, compare, and successfully purchase their selected policy within a strict 5-minute timeframe. The architecture eliminates every unnecessary step — pre-populating destination details from the search query and anchoring the policy selection above the fold.

Scannable Insurance Comparison UI

Designed a highly scannable comparison UI that standardizes complex data from providers like AXA and Zurich, instantly highlighting key metrics like HK$1,000,000 medical coverage and HK$5,600 quarantine allowances in a unified visual language. Users no longer need to read policy PDFs to compare — the table does the work for them.

COVID-19 Coverage Reassurance Checklist

Developed a specialized "COVID-19 Protection Checklist" UI component that visually maps coverage details to common user anxieties (e.g., trip cancellation limits, quarantine hotel costs, COVID-positive medical treatment). This acts as an interactive reassurance tool, converting anxiety into purchase confidence at the point of hesitation.

06 Impact

Accelerated the customer journey by enabling users to evaluate over 30 insurance plans and complete their purchase flow in under 5 minutes, directly increasing affiliate conversion rates during the peak post-pandemic travel rebound.

Increased partner conversions by prominently anchoring MoneyHero's exclusive 30% discount throughout the user journey, driving higher affiliate revenue and strengthening the platform's value proposition versus direct insurer channels.

Reduced customer support friction by proactively addressing post-pandemic travel FAQs directly within the contextual flow of the page, proving UX's value in mitigating operational overhead while simultaneously improving the user experience.
