Manulife Lead Generation Optimization

01Project Overview

Project Type
Application Funnel Optimization
Company
SingSaver (MoneyHero Group)
Team
Product Managers, Marketing
Timeline
May – June 2024

02Problem

The Manulife insurance lead generation funnel on SingSaver suffered from high form abandonment rates. Users were confronted with a long, single-page form that exposed all fields at once, creating cognitive overload before the first field was completed.

The form lacked clear value signposting and failed to communicate why each piece of information was being collected, eroding trust early in the journey. Dense legal text and eligibility requirements were buried below the fold, leading users to drop off when unexpected information surfaced mid-flow.

The business needed a redesigned funnel that could increase qualified lead submissions while maintaining compliance with Manulife's strict brand and legal guidelines.

03Research and Insights

Form Friction Analysis:

Session recordings revealed users spending excessive time on the first screen, confused by the volume of simultaneous fields. The single-column layout on mobile made the form feel endless.

Trust Signals:

Users who dropped off cited concerns about data privacy and unclear next steps. Manulife's brand credibility was underutilized — the form lacked reassurance cues at critical decision points.

Competitive Benchmark:

Leading insurance comparison platforms used chunked, step-by-step flows with progress indicators. SingSaver's single-page approach was out of step with user expectations shaped by competitors.

04Design Strategy

Bite-Sized Chunking:

Break the long form into discrete, logically grouped steps. Each screen presents only what the user needs to complete at that moment, eliminating overwhelm before the journey begins.

Momentum Building:

A visible progress indicator creates psychological commitment at each step. Users who see progress are more likely to complete the flow — the sunk cost principle working in our favour.

Transparency & Trust:

Eligibility criteria and data usage disclosures surfaced upfront in plain language, preventing mid-flow surprises. Manulife's brand credibility and logo placement added reassurance at critical decision points.

05Solution

High-Conversion CTA Strategy:

The redesign features prominent, strategically placed CTAs like “Sign Up to Win” and “Submit.” By eliminating visual noise around these touchpoints, we ensure a frictionless path to conversion.

Streamlined User Journey

We’ve replaced complexity with clarity. A numbered, step-by-step flow simplifies campaign mechanics, reducing cognitive load and allowing users to grasp the process in seconds.

Concise & Scannable Messaging

Content has been optimized for speed and transparency. By using structured, high-impact copy, we maintain user trust while ensuring the value proposition is immediately clear.

Mobile-First Excellence

The interface is fully responsive, prioritizing touch-friendly navigation and optimized input fields. The form is built for seamless interaction, ensuring a premium experience across all devices.

Reinforced Brand Credibility

To build immediate trust, the Manulife logo is now positioned prominently at the top of the hierarchy, anchoring the campaign with established brand authority.

Integrated "Blog-Friendly" Application Form

We’ve swapped bulky layouts for a compact banner card that preserves the blog’s readability. Key features include:

  • Reduced Height: Minimizes scrolling fatigue.
  • Urgency Drivers: An integrated countdown timer to boost FOMO.
  • Frictionless Design: A step-by-step guide that keeps the campaign visible without interrupting the editorial experience.

06Impact

Dramatically reduced form abandonment on mobile devices, directly impacting the bottom line.

Increased the total volume of qualified leads, proving that simplifying complex financial data directly drives customer acquisition and business growth.
